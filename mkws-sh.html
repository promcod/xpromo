Why Does Your Site Look Like A 90s Horror Show?

First of all, thank you. I'll take that as a compliment.

It's actually very much deliberate. I'm fairly familiar with CSS3 and all the new whizz bang CSS things that I could very well use to make this fancy looking, but I decided to go the simplest route.

I'm not against modern HTML and CSS. I just wanted to make a site that was debloated to the max and very much in the spirit of minimalism. Also it's basically mobile responsive out of the box. Yeah baby.
